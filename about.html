About the Owners

Woodmoore is currently owned by 2 brothers, Jaswant Singh and Surinder Singh.
Both brothers immigrated to the USA viewing it as the land of opporunities. It would turn out to be just that.
They first stayed in New York until 2008, working various jobs.
They worked in restaurants and delis before making the decision of driving the ever-famous NYC yellow taxis.
They moved from New York to Maryland in 2008 and opening their own food store here, York's Pizza Subs and Wings in Baltimore. It was a huge success.
After almost a decade, they sold it and bought Woodmoore from extended family and have been running it ever since making multiple new additions.
