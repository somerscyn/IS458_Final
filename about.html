About the Owners

Woodmoore is currently owned by two brothers, Jaswant Singh and Surinder Singh.
Both brothers immigrated to the USA, viewing it as the land of opportunityâ€”and it would turn out to be just that.
They first lived in New York until 2008, working various jobs in restaurants and delis, eventually driving the iconic NYC yellow taxis.
In 2008, they moved to Maryland and opened a successful food store: York's Pizza Subs and Wings in Baltimore.
After nearly a decade, they sold the business and purchased Woodmoore from extended family. Since then, they have made multiple new additions and continue to grow the business.
